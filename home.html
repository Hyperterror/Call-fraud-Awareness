About Us

CallGuard is a non-profit initiative dedicated to raising awareness about telephone fraud and protecting consumers from financial scams. Our team consists of cybersecurity experts, former law enforcement officers, and fraud prevention specialists.

Since our founding in 2020, we've educated over 100,000 individuals through our awareness campaigns and helped prevent millions in potential fraud losses. Our mission is to create a safer digital environment for everyone.

We partner with government agencies, financial institutions, and community organizations to stay ahead of emerging fraud tactics and provide up-to-date prevention resources.
